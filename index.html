Our focus

About us

We help women turn potential into opportunity.

Elevate Her Innovation Hub supports girls and women across Zambia with practical training, mentorship and access to networks that help them participate in the digital economy and build sustainable livelihoods.

We believe innovation is not only about technology. It is about solving real community problems with creativity, confidence and collaboration.

Our Mission

To equip girls and women with skills, confidence and support to thrive as innovators, entrepreneurs and leaders.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every girl and woman has the opportunity to learn, create, earn and lead.

Our Values

Inclusion, dignity, courage, innovation, accountability and community impact.

What we do

Programs designed for practical impact

Digital Skills Bootcamps

Hands-on training in computer basics, online safety, productivity tools, coding foundations and digital entrepreneurship.

Girls in STEM Clubs

School and community clubs that expose girls to science, technology, engineering and problem-solving careers.

Women Entrepreneurs Lab

Business training, market access support, branding, financial literacy and mentorship for women-led ventures.

Mentorship Network

Connecting young women with role models, professionals and entrepreneurs who can guide their growth.
